How it works

You join the club and show up to weekly runs along Barcelona's coast, guided by music and good energy. Each session is open to all levels — no pressure, no competition. Just a consistent rhythm, a supportive crew, and the simple joy of running together.

What we offer

We offer weekly group runs 🏃‍♂️ along Barcelona's coast 🌊, fueled by music 🔊, movement, and good company 🤝. Every session includes water 💧, assistance 🆘, and the support of a crew that runs with you — not ahead of you. You'll also get access to exclusive challenges 🎯, community events 🎉, and members-only drops 🧢 designed to keep you moving.

Our main route

We start at Marina Diagonal and glide down to Diagonal Mar, cutting through the pulse of the city. From there, we hit the beachfront — wide open and alive — and follow the coast all the way to the iconic Hotel W. It's rhythm, skyline, and sea breeze — the perfect route to lock in your pace and clear your mind.
