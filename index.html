How does it works

Join weekly runs along Barcelona's coast, led by our team and powered by music. Show up, move at your own pace, and connect with others who run for rhythm, not records. No pressure, no competition — just consistency, energy, and good vibes.

What we offer

Weekly group runs along Barcelona's coast, guided by energy and good music. A supportive crew that helps you stay consistent, no matter your pace or experience. Access to exclusive challenges, community events, and members-only drops.

Our main route

We start at Marina Diagonal and glide down to Diagonal Mar, cutting through the pulse of the city. From there, we hit the beachfront — wide open and alive — and follow the coast all the way to the iconic Hotel W. It's rhythm, skyline, and sea breeze — the perfect route to lock in your pace and clear your mind.
